The Maybelline Company was created by a 19 year old entrepreneur named tom lyle williams in 1915. williams noticed his older sister mabel applying a mixture of vaseline and coal dust to her eyelashes to give them a darker and fuller look. he took inspiration from this and produced the product locally. later he changed the name to maybelline to honour his sister. In 1991, the company adopted its advertising slogan “(Maybe she's born with it.) Maybe it's Maybelline.” which is still used today. The company was acquired by L'Oréal in 1996. Acquiring Maybelline gave L'Oréal access to mass markets in cosmetics.
